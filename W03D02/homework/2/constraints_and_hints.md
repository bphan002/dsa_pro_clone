#  Maximum 69 Number Constraints and Hints

## Constraints
-   `1 <= num <= 10^4`
-   `num consists of only 6 and 9 digits.`

## Hint #1:
> Convert the number in an array of its digits.

## Hint #2: 
> Would it ever make sense for us to change a 9 to a 6?

## Hint #3:
> How can we change a digit such that we get the biggest digit possible?
